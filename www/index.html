About

Long ago, I played soccer and tennis with my friends near Salzburg (Austria), before I decided to move on to Graz (Austria) in order to pursue my Bachelor's and Master's degree from Graz University of Technology in 2009 and 2011, respectively. Currently, I am working towards my Ph.D. at the Integrated Systems Laboratory at ETH Zurich (Switzerland) in the Digital Circuits and Systems group. My research interests include efficient hardware implementations of cryptographic primitives for both ASIC and FPGA platforms. Moreover, I am interested in hardware Trojans and their detection as well as in securing the development chain of SoCs in general.
